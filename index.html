About

Goshen Albion Ltd is a privately owned UK company managed by a single director. It operates as a proprietary entity, managing its own capital for long-term treasury and investment purposes.

The company does not provide services, investment advice, or asset management to external clients and does not hold or manage funds on behalf of third parties.

Activities

  • Managing and allocating company-owned capital
  • Holding diversified financial assets via regulated platforms
  • Maintaining cash reserves for treasury purposes
  • Occasional capital reallocation as part of long-term strategy

Important Notice

This company does not provide financial advice, does not operate as an asset manager, does not accept client funds, and does not offer services or products to the public. All activity is conducted strictly on a proprietary basis using company funds only.

Regulatory Position

The company is not authorised or regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). It does not conduct regulated investment services or hold client money.

Source of Funds

Initial and ongoing funding is provided by director capital contributions derived from personal savings.

Future Plans

The company may expand its activities over time as capital grows, including diversification across additional asset classes. All activities will remain proprietary in nature.
